Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving creativity and technology for economic growth and societal advancement.

Sustainability

Ensuring resource efficiency, renewable energy, and environmental responsibility.

Infrastructure

Developing modern systems to connect, support, and sustain communities.

Empowerment

Providing skills, education, and opportunities to shape better futures.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Each of us has much more hidden inside of us than we have had a chance to explore. Unless we create an environment that enables us to discover the limits of our potential, we will never know what we have. I strongly believe that we can create a poverty-free world, if we collectively believe in it. In a poverty-free world, the only place you can see poverty is in a museum. When you visit a museum, you will see an exhibit on poverty, and you will be horrified to see how people in the past suffered unnecessarily. A poverty-free world might not be perfect, but it would be the best we could achieve as human beings."
Dr. Muhammad Yunus
Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
